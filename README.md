## :star2: About the Project
<p>SaladPunk. A surreal, branching Visual Novel (VN) littered with easter eggs, hidden systems, meta-narrative elements, and multiple intertwining storylines. <br />It is about helping James — and yourself — find Salad... but maybe you find something more?<br />
  And for part two, you take a dive into the sands of time, experiencing John's childhood second-hand, though the eyes of his older sibling.<br />
Who is John? Well you've got to play the game and pay attention to find that out.</p>
